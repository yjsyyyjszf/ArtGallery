Our Mission

Pinpoint the issues with how post-internet, digitally-created works are subjected to sharing, duplication and version slippage because of the way they are created, exhibited, streamed and distributed. Examine modes of authorship and authentication + apply them to blockchain technology. Engage stakeholder institutions, artists and artist-supporting organizations to create a transformational tool for digital artists to authenticate work and oversee the ways in which they are viewed/distributed
